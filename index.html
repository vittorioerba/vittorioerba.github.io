Vittorio Erba

Ph.D. student in Statistical Mechanics @ UNIMI

I'm a Ph.D. student in Statistical Mechanics at the University of Milan, where I work under the supervision of Professor Sergio Caracciolo.

My research projects lie at the intersection of disordered-systems physics, machine learning and combinatorial optimization. At the moment, my interest is shifting more and more on the study of learning architectures.

When I'm not programming for research, you can usually find me programming for fun, cooking or reading a good book!
