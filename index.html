I'm a Ph.D. student in Statistical Mechanics at the University of Milan, where I work under the supervision of Professor Sergio Caracciolo.

I'm interested in how Euclidean and non-Euclidean metric structures affect the collective behaviour of large numbers of interacting degrees of freedom, both in low and high dimension and in presence of disorder.
