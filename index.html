Vittorio Erba - CV - Mail

Hi! I am a postodoctoral researcher at the EPFL (Lausanne), where I work at the intersection of statistical physics, high dimensional statistics and machine learning in the group of Lenka Zdeborová. My research is funded through a SNSF Postdoctoral Fellowship. Before, I got my PhD at the Università degli Studi in Milano, working on statistical physics of combinatorial problems and data science under the supervision of Sergio Caracciolo.

When I'm not doing research, you can usually find me playing music, doing some sports or reading a good book!

Publications: the best way to get an updated list of my publications is the arχiv , where you can also access them without paywalls. Other useful academic profiles are Google Scholar , Publons , Scopus and Orcid .

Software: you can find some software I wrote (for my research or not) on Github or on the SPOC lab Github .
